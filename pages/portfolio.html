On My Dot

An app to assist marching band students create their Dot Books for use during their competitive season
This was my final project for the UCF Coding Boot Camp.
Students can enter their coordinates from the drill charts provided by their band director.
This can replace more archaic methods like writing it all down in index card form.
This way if things change, there is no reason to rewrite or replace the cards.
Technologies used: Laravel 5.3, HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, JavaScript, JQuery
